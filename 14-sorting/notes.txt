Sorting: notes

- Naive sorting algorithms take O(n^2) time
- O(nlog(n)) algorithms: heapsort, merge sort, quicksort
  - Heapsort: in-place but not stable
  - Merge sort: stable but not in-place
  - Quicksort: O(n^2) time in worst case

- in-place sort: uses O(1) space
- stable sort: equal entries appear in original orders
